Smart Real Estate Price Analysis.

Project Domain:- Easte Analytics| Data Analysis | Business Intelligence

Project Objective:-

  • The objective of this project is to analyze housing market data to identify key factors influencing house prices, clean real-world messy data containing missing values, and present visual insights that support data-driven decision making for real-estate businesses, buyers, and investors.

Skills

  1. Python
  2. Pandas
  3. Numpy
  4. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
  5. Missing Value Treatment.
  6. Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA)
  7. Data Visulization (Matplotlib & Seaborn)
  8. Business Insight Generation

Dataset Description

  • Dataset Name: King County House Sales Dataset (with missing values)
  • Records: 21,613
  • Features: 22
  • Target Variable: price

Key Attributes:

  • Property size (sqft_living, sqft_lot)
  • Structural details (bedrooms, bathrooms, floors)
  • Quality indicators (grade, condition)
  • Location features (zipcode, waterfront, view)
  • Renovation and construction data

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

Q1.What is the distrubution of house prices?

In[16]:
plt.figure()
plt.hist(df['price'], bins=30)
plt.title("Distrubution of House Prices")
plt.xlabel("Price")
plt.ylabel("Frequency")
plt.show()

Analysis :-

  • Distrubution is Right Skewed.
  • Majority houses are in tha mid price range

Conclusion.

  • Luxury houses are fewer, market is domainated by affordable properties.

Q2. How does living area affect price?

In[18]:
plt.scatter(df['sqft_living'], df['price'])
plt.xlabel("Sqft of Living")
plt.ylabel("Price")
plt.title("Price vc Living Area")
plt.show()

Analysis :-

  • Strong positive correlation.

Conclusion.

  • Bigger homes generally cost more.

Q3. What is the relationship between bedrooms and Price.

In[22]:
sns.boxplot(x = 'bedrooms', y='price', data=df)
plt.title("Bedrooms vc Price")
plt.show()

Analysis :-

  • Price increases till certain bedroom count.

Conclusion:-

  • After 6 bedrooms, price groth slows.

Q4. Do bathrooms impack house price?

  • Strong impact than bathrooms.

Conclusion:-

  • Bathrooms are more important than bedrooms for pricing.

Q5.Does waterfront view increase price?

Q6.What is the correlation among key variables?
